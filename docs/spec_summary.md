# COMP90051 Project Spec Summary

Source: `comp90051_26sem1_project.pdf`.

## Due Dates

- Optional Canvas submission window: Sunday 26 April 2026 11:59pm to Tuesday 5
  May 2026 11:59pm for clarifying questions/plans and group agreement.
- Final Canvas submission: Tuesday 26 May 2026 11:59pm.

## Final Submission

- 4-page PDF research report.
- ZIP archive of Python code or notebooks, a short `Readme.txt`, and automation
  scripts.
- Optional Slack/GitHub logs or meeting minutes.
- Do not submit data.

## Required Methods

- Public dataset(s), with URLs in the report.
- Non-trivial feature construction/preprocessing.
- Three distinct algorithms with simple, medium, and complex model classes.
- At least one algorithm from a qualifying 2016-or-later research paper in
  NeurIPS, ICML, UAI, AISTATS, JMLR, ICLR, or TMLR.
- Same outer cross-validation method for all algorithms.
- Cross-validation from scratch.
- Hyperparameter tuning from scratch.
- Nested cross-validation from scratch.
- Experimental metrics from scratch.
- At least three reported results with error bars.

## Report Sections

1. Student IDs and names.
2. Introduction: research question, dataset(s), URLs.
3. Literature review: dataset references and at least two additional papers.
4. Methods: feature construction, preprocessing, algorithms, CV, tuning.
5. Results and discussion: tables/charts, analysis, alternatives considered.
6. Conclusion.
7. Bibliography.

## Format Rules

- Maximum 4 pages total, including references and appendices.
- Single column.
- A4 page size.
- 10pt font.
- 1.5cm to 2cm margins.
